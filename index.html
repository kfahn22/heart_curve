There are many ways to draw a heart shape. It could be something really simple like rotating a square by PI/4 and attatching half circles to two adjacent sides. You can also render a heart using higher order math equations. Following [Daniel Shiffman](https://thecodingtrain.com)'s approach in his Heart Curve coding challenge, I choose the later route. I utilized the equations from two different websites. In some cases, I had to tinker a bit with the parameters until I was able to render a decent heart shape. The code for the heart sketches can be found here.
